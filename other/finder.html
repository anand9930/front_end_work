WHO ARE OUR FINDERS
INDUSTRY SPECIALIST WITH SECTORAL EXPERIENCE AND NETWORKS.
Sourcak Finders and consultants are our business partners who help us scout for buyers or sellers of a product or help us with private label strategy and setting up of projects, of which they have relevant sectorial experience. Finders introduce new leads and business opportunities regularly to the Sourcak team either via attending trade shows or their past networks. Supply side finders keep submitting details of pre-vetted suppliers or verify existing suppliers for us who can produce quality goods and services at competitive prices.
Some of our finders are stay at home mom or people on a sabbatical/career shift who used to work at marketing consultants, sourcing managers, professors at ivy league schools, consultants at top consultancy firms, lawyers at magic circle law firms and final year students wanting to build a career in trade, representing top 1% of their class. The flexibility to work your own hours and the convenience of operating from home is what attracts them often to our platform.
As our business partners, the specialist share the profits we earn on closing the transaction be it a project export or a procurement opportunity for a tender awarded to a client via our network.
They add value by doing inspections, finding reasonable prices, fostering good trade relationship and help in closing of the sale.

Sourcak connects pre-vetted buyers to pre-vetted sellers via its network of finders, and on the other hand, Sourcak’s consultants also help with private labeling and factory setup process. We mainly focus on 3 domains, i.e., Product Development, Product Sourcing, and Factory Set Up. Our network of finders identify high-quality suppliers who are otherwise difficult to find and buyers from around the world and our consultants help with private labeling and factory set up process.
Our finders are professionals with an average experience of 4-5 year in their sector. They range from self-employed individuals, ex sourcing managers, large startup founders, ex-consultants of Mckinsey and Bain, product experts, factory set up consultants and in exceptional cases final year students who are in the top 1% of their class in ivy league schools.

Sourcak finders are the core of our business. We treat them as business partners and add value to their professional life, and they add value to us by sharing valuable pre-vetted connections in different industries. With our current network of 1700 finders in over 40 countries, we can operate on a much larger scale than other sourcing platforms. This provides us the ability to provide global sourcing at a fast pace.If you are interested in becoming a Finder with Sourcak, we encourage you to fill out your details here.
A finder associated with Sourcak can add value in one of the following manners:

Associate Suppliers with Sourcak
Being a global sourcing company, we always look for trustworthy suppliers that can help provide good quality products at the most suitable prices. Sourcak asks their finders to get quotes of different products from their existing pre-vetted suppliers and identify new suppliers for the same.

Connect Buyers to Sourcak
Sourcak finders can also help by connecting us to buyers of different products. Finders will get upto 75% of the commission that is generated from the respective transaction on introducing a particular buyer. Our team will do everything from sourcing, logistics, etc. We merely need you to stay involved to smoothen the process.

Sharing Expertise with Sourcak
Our consultants are experts in a sector who help with private labeling of products and with the factory setup process, with a rich history of over 10 years in that sector. We also invite founders of successful startup to become consultants with us to help others private label products in that category.
Working as a Sourcak Finder is a part-time entrepreneurship opportunity that anyone can take up. We reward you for your knowledge and connection. A Sourcak Finder can work from anywhere in the world regardless of their location. We, however, expect our finders to work with the utmost professionalism by submitting high-grade work and respect deadlines.
Usually, people who have 3-4 years of work experience in any industry tend to be qualified to become Sourcak Finders. We make exceptions sometimes for highly motivated people with exceptional achievements. Since our finders are our business partners, many view this as an entrepreneurship opportunity with little risk on your side. Our application process is an informal written conversation where we get to know you. We encourage you to talk about your business networks and experience in sales and sourcing. This will help us determine how much value can we add to your professional life and how well can you assist our clients.
You can earn from Sourcak on the following conversions:

Introduce suppliers: Upto 25% of the commission for each successful deal that takes place from the supplier you recommended with our buyers.

Introduce buyers: Upto 75% of the total commission on the transaction that takes place between a buyer introduced by you and Sourcak.

Introduce New Products: You will also be able to earn by introducing new products to the Sourcak database which are presently not there. For every new product, you can get upto 150 dollars when approved and added.

Providing Expertise: You can sign up as a consultant who has past experience in setting up of factories or private labeling products or introduce potential clients looking for such services. Upon conversation you earn upto 50% of the total commission.
There are two ways through which Sourcak can pay you for the work done:

  • Directly into your bank account via bank transfer.
  • Into your PayPal account. [Recommended]

As a Sourcak Finder, you connect us with the buyers you are connected to, with details of products they are looking at sourcing regardless of their location or product category. Opening lines of communication between Sourcak and the buyer, helps both the parties discover new trade opportunities. We have access to rich trade data which has over 1 billion trade records, across the world. Using technology and feet on the ground, we are able to get good terms and quotes from pre-vetted suppliers. You will earn upto 75% commission earned via this trade, by assisting us in the process till the end by negotiating pricing and terms. Most of the other work such as sourcing, product sampling and development, logistics etc will be handled by our team.
As a Finder for Sourcak, you have to connect a supplier to our network by sharing information about them including their quote of specific items.This networking helps past or potential suppliers explore new trade. For registering a valid supplier with Sourcak, you get upto 25% of the commission of all trades made by that supplier.

You can also consider attending one of the many trade shows of a particular product to get this information. You can submit your findings to us in the form of google sheets.
You must submit as much information as possible including details of the suppliers, their brochures, quotes which are detailed with terms such as payment terms, logistics terms, quality and other certificates. If possible list of past supplies also assist us in evaluating supplies.

Often we post consultancy jobs relating to other areas such as exports, imports, procurement etc.
Depending on the nature of the job, the expectations can greatly vary. Incase the project involves setting up of a factory then we expect you to submit a project report or draft one after we get an approval from the client. You might be asked to answer a set of questions or consult the client either in person or on telephone mostly in case of product development.
