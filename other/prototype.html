Tridge is a global platform to connect local suppliers with buyers through the collaboration of our network of professionals.

We run a global sourcing network.
We invest our own capital to develop the best logistics.
We buy and manage inventories on our own account.
We make business with buyers who are discovered by Tridge or our Finders.
Who are our Finders

Local specialists with seasoned industry knowledge and network

Finders make our global sourcing network possible . They are business partners who have experience and have built their own network in the region. Finders are compensated for sharing their market intelligence.
The value of their contribution lays on their understanding of their local market and ability to find reasonable prices for our buyers and building strong relations with our partners.
