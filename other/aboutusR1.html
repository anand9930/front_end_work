Why do we exist?
Procurement managers across the world spend countless hours flying across the world, making multiple trial order only to delay the speed of the project. Countless online marketplaces have sprung up in the last decade with millions of registered suppliers and with it came an army of scamsters. Apart from that risk of fraud, there is also the tedious job of having to go through 100s of similar quotes. We have developed a unique model for cross border procurement which will be patented shortly, where we use artificial intelligence, past trade records, industry trends, and pricing worldwide and our army of finders to help recommend the best supplier for you. Our team starts with the process of request for quotes which our army of finders take up along with the procurement team. We target the top 1% of manufacturers and traders in various categories. We use in-house and third-party verification agency where we can submit factory photos and also undertake process inspection as against just a final quality template like a certificate which is currently being supplied by manufacturers. We will help you find reliable suppliers which will further result in cost and time savings.
